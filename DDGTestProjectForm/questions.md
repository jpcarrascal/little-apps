## Problem statement

Please walk us through a significant project or initiative where you were in a lead role or were a key contributor. Choose an example that you're proud of, and that best illustrates your project management abilities, ideally from the last 2 years (but feel free to go beyond that if that allows for a better example).

## Intro

0. Begin by describing the project and the team that worked on it, your role on that team, and then tell the story all the way through to a retrospective, including an assessment of the impact the project actually had.

High level, we would like to understand:
* The context of the project, including your investigation of problems that were encountered and your approach to solving them.
* How you constructed the project plan and determined success criteria.
* The timeline of the project and how it was delivered.
* Key decisions in the project, how you approached them, and what you learned.
* The impact of that project and the role you played in driving it across the finish line.
* How you interacted with cross functional stakeholders.
* What delivery best practices you found particularly valuable.
* What traps you fell into or wrong-turns you took.
* What lessons you learned and what changes were made resulting from those lessons (if any).

## Questions

1. Explain the rationale for the project and whether it had the expected impact. Please include:
* What data (if any) indicated it was worth pursuing?
* How did you define and measure success? What KPIs did you set?
* What complex decisions did you make in the project?
* What was the final outcome? Please include relevant data (if any).
Your answer should be 500 words or fewer.

2. Talk us through your thought process on one of the decisions you described above. Please include:
* What options did you consider and how did you evaluate them?
* What steps did you take to de-risk the downsides of the selected solution?
* What outcome did that decision have?
Your answer should be 500 words or fewer.

3. How did you approach delivery in this project?
* How did you scope the delivery of the project?
* Was there anything you prioritised or tried to deliver earlier?
* How did it work relative to your expectations?
Your answer should be 350 words or fewer.

4. What were your biggest individual learnings? How would you apply them if given the chance to redo that project? 
Your answer should be 250 words or fewer.

5. Did you use an LLM to complete any part of this test project submission? If yes, please briefly explain how. It's especially helpful to see the prompts you employed, but you can also disclose general use cases, such as researching particular topics or improving writing quality.
